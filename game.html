Game Instructions

Welcome to Tank Battle Royale! The aim of the game is to destroy the enemy tank by reducing its health to zero. The floating health bar above each tank shows how much damage has been inflicted. The game ends when your or the enemy tank's health is reduced to zero.

Controls

  • Drive forward: up arrow
  • Drive backward: down arrow
  • Rotate left: left arrow
  • Rotate right: right arrow
  • Move turret: "a" / "d" keys
  • Fire: space bar

Press p to pause the game

Cheat

Press h to add 100 health!
